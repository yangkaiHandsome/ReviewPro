# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to keep discussions respectful, technical, and constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- discuss ideas directly and in good faith
- focus feedback on code, design, and behavior
- accept correction without escalation
- avoid harassment, insults, and personal attacks

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- personal abuse or intimidation
- discriminatory language or behavior
- doxxing or sharing private information
- repeated bad-faith disruption of discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors that violate these standards.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and project communication spaces.
